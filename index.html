About Me

Hello! I'm Zahan Badshah, a passionate computer science student from Conestoga College in Waterloo, Ontario. With a solid grounding in programming languages like C and C++, I'm always eager to dive into new tech challenges. My objective is to blend my technical aptitude with my strong customer engagement skills, fostering collaboration and adaptability in any work environment.

More About Me

Born and raised in India, I completed my high school studies in Science from Mithibai College before venturing to Canada for my Bachelor's in Computer Science. My journey in the tech world has been diverse. From teaching coding and 3D design to young students at Steamoji in Mississauga to assisting customers at Love Sugar and Dough in Mumbai, I've always been driven by a desire to learn and make an impact.

One of my proudest achievements is a personal project where I developed a motion-sensing catapult using an Arduino. This project was a testament to my ability to integrate hardware programming with 3D design, creating a responsive and functional system.

Outside of academics and work, I'm an avid tech enthusiast, always staying updated with the latest in the industry. When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new places or diving into a good book.
